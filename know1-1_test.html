Lesson 1: Our God is Wonderful

QUESTION PAGE

Instructions:

  • First, enter the information to identify yourself — your User ID and password.
  • Then go through the questions and click on the best answer for each question.
  • When you are finished, click on the "submit" button to send your answers.

     

  

Questions:

(Select the best answer for each question)

1. Long before there were any flowers, birds or trees, God already existed.

    a) True.

    b) False.

2. God wanted to make the earth beautiful especially for

    a) birds and the animals.

    b) His children to live in.

3. Did God make the trees?

    a) Yes.

    b) No.

4. Why did God make night time?

    a) So that boys and girls could have sleep and rest.

    b) So that moms and dads could have sleep and rest.

    c) Both of the above.

5. What did God make to light up the night time?

    a) The moon.

    b) The twinkling stars.

    c) Both of the above.

6. Did God make the snow?

    a) Yes.

    b) No.

7. Is God still making beautiful things for us to enjoy?

    a) Yes.

    b) No.

8. When you look at a beautiful sunset or at the starry sky at night do you think of all the beautiful things that God has made for us to enjoy?

    a) Yes.

    b) No.

9. Do you thank God for all that he has made for you to enjoy?

    a) Yes.

    b) No.
